Welcome to *ehrror*, by Justin Ehringhaus. This is my canvas for weaving thoughts, ideas, and discoveries. This space is born from a fascination with the beauty of languages, the power of technology, and the hidden patterns in data. 

Just as the intricacies of a language can reveal the depths of a culture, so too can the patterns in data reveal the underlying narratives of our world. I hope to break down the barriers between us and these narratives.
