Four years ago, I began furiously coding up 'hello-world's like a possum on sweet potato. Not long after, potatoless, I was diving headlong into neural nets with Coursera's Andrew Ng.

The allure of grandiosity is a powerful one, but it's also a vice. The real art is in not losing sight of simple joys — the ones that exist in coding a line that works, in crafting a sentence that sings, in savoring a moment of pure living.

This is what *ehrror* represents. A digital playground by Justin Ehringhaus for both the careful and the carefree exploration of ideas.
